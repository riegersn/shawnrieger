about me

Hi, I'm Shawn.

Father. Husband. Impassioned developer with a hearty appetite for learning new things.

Born in Texas, grew up in South Jersey. Received my AAS in Computer Information Systems at Camden County College. When I'm not coding I'm spending time with my wife and 5 year old son.

Intrigued by photography, woodworking, and drawing. Enjoy fall and winter hiking on the Batona trail, a 53 mile hiking trail through the Jersey Pine Barrens and others.

If I wasn't a developer? I'd do something with my hands, be my own boss. A handyman most likely. I have a knack for fixing just about anything. Youtube has saved me thousands of dollars over the years.

Got the bug for development in the mid-90's. You know... Juno, CompuServe, America Online. Teaching myself Visual Basic, I developed an "auto re-dialer" for AOL utilizing SendKeys, the line was always busy in the evenings. It's difficult to describe what I felt back then. Enchanted, amazed, filled with wonder and curiosity.

I still feel that curiosity today. It's one of the things that drives me to continue coding and learning new things.

work

I started my career in the tech field working for Commerce Bank and TD Bank NA as an IT Help Desk agent. I got my foot in the door but it wasn't my passion. I continued coding in VB6 on the side for fun until early 2009, I discovered the small startup Boxee.

Boxee was a desktop "media-center" software turned set-top device based off XBMC, currating video content from around the web. Their software supported building applications using XUL and Python. I quickly built a PBS and GameTrailers app. Threw up BoxeeHQ.com to host my applications in a repository.

Come mid-June of 09, my apps where gaining traction with the community, when I received a email from the Boxee guys about my work. I joined the Boxee team and continued to write apps for the platform over the next 4 years.
